SmartRover Project

The SmartRover is an open source project that is revised and based on the SmartCar project in so far as providing open source software and hardware components accompanied by integrated libraries for optimal usage. In contrast to the SmartCar project which provides a terrain and a miniature vehicle the SmartRover is further developed and customised to explore and accomplish mission tasks on a martian terrain. The project is designed to resemble and offer a close to real-life experience on how a martian exploration could be whilst providing leeway for the user in terms of having mission tasks and functionalities at their disposal.

Manual Control

In the manual control page you are able to control the rover using your mouse and the keyboard, you can get serial feed of the SmartRover current action, visual feedback and the SmartRover current statues as well.

Manual control page contains six parts:

LIVE STREAM

is a visual feedback from the camera that is located in the front of the SmartRover. Which makes it easier to avoid obstacles that are in the rover's way.

CONTROL PAD

It's never been easier to control another thing than our control pad. In the control pad it's easy to start/shut down the SmartRover, direct the SmartRover, take screenshot from the camera feedback, connect the SmartRover to the server and enable/disable obstacle avoidance.

TELEMETRY

In the telemetry you can get the current and actual heading, speed, throttle, turn angle and the total distance that the SmartRover cross.

TERMINAL

In the SmartRover's Terminal you are able to control the rover by typing commands using your keyboard, and here is the executable command list:

  • help
  • hi
  • .

SERIAL FEED

Serial feed is updating you with serial feed of the actions that the rover taking at the same moment.

Live Streaming

You can get live visual feedback from the SmartRover. The live streaming makes it easier to see the environment in Mars planet and avoid obstacles while controlling the SmartRover.
